We help students build the future

JU MakerSpace is Rajasthan's first official makerspace, inaugurated on 16 August 2018 at JECRC University by Mr. Hastikaji, Vice President, TCS.

What started with one student has now grown into a thriving community of hundreds across different fields.

Students at MakerSpace work on 3D printing, robotics, electronics, software, art, hardware, design, woodworking, and more—making it India's most diverse student-driven innovation ecosystem.

The only rule at MakerSpace: No limits on imagination.
